KlipPay: Stablecoin Payment Platform

AhnLab Blockchain Company | May - July 2025

Overview

As a Product Development Intern, I worked on a new stablecoin-based service called KlipPay. I led a team of five members through the entire product development process, from ideation and market research to UI/UX design and the final presentation.

The Problem

The main problem we identified with traditional payment systems is that they involve multiple intermediaries, which leads to high transaction fees, complex approval processes, and delayed settlements. We saw an opportunity to use stablecoins to address this problem.

Market Opportunity

  • In the U.S., Tether and Circle hold a dominant 94% market share
  • Circle's recent NASDAQ listing demonstrated that stablecoin companies can generate significant revenue, with their primary model being interest income from U.S. Treasury bond reserves
  • In Korea, the government's proposed Digital Asset Basic Act whcih signals an imminent market opening for stablecoins, with major banks and fintechs already preparing trademarks

My Role & The Solution

I contributed to the product design, focusing on UI/UX and developing key marketing strategies. Our solution, KlipPay, reduces transaction friction with lower fees, real-time settlements, and a transparent transaction history.

Product Design (UI/UX)

Users can link bank accounts, send money to friends, or pay by scanning a QR code

Automatically accumulates digital stamps with each payment without sign ups

Users set monthly personal goals, rewards increase with higher success rates, SNS challenges and shareable badges to encourage participation

Key Takeaways

Through this project, I was able to learn the entire process of planning and designing a new product within a company, from conducting competitor and market analysis to turning ideas into reality and even developing marketing strategies. I also learned the importance of effective collaboration and clear communication when working in a team setting.

I enjoyed combining my technical skills and creativity to transform new ideas into viable products. Through this internship, I found my passion for pursuing a career in product management.
